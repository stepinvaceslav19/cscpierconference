Author information
Dear colleagues!
We invite you to participate in the 21th International Workshop Complex Systems of Charged Particles and Their Interactions with Electromagnetic Radiation (CSCPIER-2025), which will be held in Prokhorov General Physics Institute of the Russian Academy of Sciences in Moscow on April 7‒11, 2025. Workshop will be held as a hybrid event (online and offline).
Important dates
January 19, 2026 – deadline of registration with a report
February 02, 2026 – deadline of registration with a report
February 23, 2026 – deadline for abstracts submission
March 1, 2026 – deadline of registration without a report.

Working languages 
Russian and English.

Converence fees
Our workshop is free of charge for all participants

Abstract submission
To publish the abstracts of your report, you must follow the instructions to issue abstracts using the template. Send the completed abstracts to the Organizing committee's e-mail cscpier@mail.ru until February 25, 2025, together with an expert opinion on the possibility of publication in the open press and a license agreement for the gratuitous transfer of non-exclusive copyright for the publication of abstracts in the book of abstracts in printed and electronic form.

Presentation

The presentation format: oral presentation using a presentation file in ppt, pptx or pdf format. The presentation time is
invited reports - 25 minutes + 5 minutes for answering questions;
oral reports - 12 minutes + 3 minutes for answering questions/

The presentation file language is English, the speech language is English or Russian.
